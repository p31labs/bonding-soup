artifact 01
geometry · the central thesis · 4 panels in lockstep

The Same Shape — K₄ at four orders of magnitude

The same minimum-complete graph (4 vertices, 6 edges, no central hub) rendered as a family mesh, a Platonic tetrahedron, a SIC-POVM in d=2, and a Posner phosphate cluster. Drag any panel — all four rotate in lockstep, because they ARE the same object. Click any vertex — the corresponding vertex highlights in the other three.

  • scale: human → geometric → quantum → chemical
  • same vertices, same edges, same symmetry group
  • replaces the original k4-mesh.html demo
  • keyboard: ← → ↑ ↓ to rotate, 1-4 to select a vertex, space to pause

artifact 02

The Pulse — one number, every screen

The Larmor frequency of phosphorus-31 (863 Hz) lives in p31-constants.json. Edit it on the page. Watch the pulse rate change. Watch the alignment DAG light up as the wave propagates from source to leaves. Watch 20+ surfaces across the codebase update their displayed value in the same window.

  • scroll-narrative: source → derivation (DAG) → manifestation (leaf grid)
  • editable canonical constant with live propagation
  • consolidates the original larmor-pulse.html + alignment-graph.html
  • this is what Buckminster Fuller called ephemeralization, made into a verb
