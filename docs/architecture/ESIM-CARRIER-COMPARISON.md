## 2. COVERAGE VERDICT: 31558 AND 32211

**Camden County GA (31558) — Saint Marys:**
- AT&T: **Good** — AT&T has stronger rural Georgia coverage than T-Mobile in general. Camden County is moderately rural; AT&T towers serve the I-95 corridor well.
- T-Mobile: **Adequate** — T-Mobile has expanded rural coverage post-Sprint merger. Coverage at 31558 is workable but can thin out away from I-95 and US-17.
- Verizon: **Good** — Best rural coverage nationally, but the Visible plan at $25 is over budget.

**Jacksonville FL (32211):**
- All major carriers: **Excellent** — Jacksonville is a major metro. T-Mobile, AT&T, and Verizon all have full coverage. No issues.

**I-95 corridor between 31558 and 32211:**
- AT&T: Consistent coverage along I-95 in this stretch.
- T-Mobile: Also consistent on I-95, slightly more variable off-highway.

**Coverage verdict:** If budget allows only one choice, **AT&T network** (via US Mobile GSMA line or Red Pocket AT&T) gives better confidence in Camden County rural areas. T-Mobile is acceptable if you stay near I-95/US-17.

**Activation steps for iPhone 11 (eSIM, no physical SIM needed):**
1. Go to `usmobile.com` → "Get Started"

4. Enter ZIP 31558 for coverage check
5. Pay with debit card (no credit card required)
6. Receive eSIM QR code via email within minutes
7. On iPhone: Settings → Cellular → Add Cellular Plan → Scan QR Code
8. Label it "US Mobile" — set as primary for calls/SMS if needed
9. Test with a call and a text before the May 14 hearing
