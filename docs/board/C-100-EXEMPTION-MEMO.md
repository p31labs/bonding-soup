## Summary

---

## Statutory Basis

The Georgia Charitable Solicitations Act (O.C.G.A. § 43-17-1 et seq.) requires charitable organizations to register with the Georgia Secretary of State before soliciting contributions from Georgia residents (O.C.G.A. § 43-17-5).

**Exemption (O.C.G.A. § 43-17-9(a)):** An organization is exempt from registration if:

1. The organization did not receive contributions exceeding **$25,000** during its immediately preceding fiscal year; AND
2. The organization does not compensate any person primarily to conduct solicitations.
