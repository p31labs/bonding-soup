## Purpose

This policy establishes standards for the retention and destruction of corporate records of P31 Labs, Inc. It applies to all records in any format — paper, electronic, or other media — and to all directors, officers, employees, volunteers, and contractors.

Destruction of records is prohibited when litigation, audit, or governmental investigation is pending or reasonably anticipated.

## Digital Records

Digital records stored in the following systems are subject to this policy:

- **GitHub (p31labs/andromeda):** Code and documentation — retained indefinitely in version control. Do not force-delete commits.
- **Cloudflare:** Worker logs auto-purged per Cloudflare policy (typically 7 days). Operator may export to R2 for longer retention.
- **Email:** Retain grant-related, legal, and financial emails consistent with the schedule above. Forward to corporate records folder.

---

## Destruction Procedure

At the expiration of the applicable retention period:

1. The President or Secretary reviews records scheduled for destruction.
2. Confirm no litigation hold or pending audit applies.
3. Electronic records: delete and confirm deletion from backup.
4. Paper records: shred or burn documents containing personal information; recycle the rest.
5. Log the destruction: date, record type, approximate volume, method, and name of person who authorized.

**No records shall be destroyed once litigation, audit, or government investigation is pending or reasonably anticipated.** Any director or officer who becomes aware of such a situation must notify the President immediately.
