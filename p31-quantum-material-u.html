03 · Cards

Tone-elevated · vertex-emphasised · asymmetric · atmosphere

Default .p31-q-card is a tone-1 surface with an elev-1 shadow. Add data-p31-tone for K4 vertex emphasis, data-p31-shape="asymmetric" for the Material 3 expressive corner pattern, or compose with .p31-q-atmosphere for an opt-in vertex glow on focus-within.

Default card

Calm at rest. Hover or focus lifts shadow + adds a state-layer overlay.

Phosphorus tone

Belonging vertex. Quietly anchors confirmed-state cards.

Coral · asymmetric

Warmth vertex with the Material 3 clipped corner. Reads as a quantum vertex anchor.

Lavender · atmosphere

K4 vertex glow appears only on focus-within or hover. Off under reduced motion.

04 · Buttons

Filled · tonal · outlined · text · vertex toned

State-layer pseudo-element (::after) overlays currentColor at Material-3 opacities for hover (8%), focus (12%), pressed (16%). Disabled drops to 38% opacity per Material 3.

05 · FAB

Floating Action Buttons

One per view; Material 3 etiquette. P31 reads it as the operator-summoned focal beat — never auto-loaded chrome.
