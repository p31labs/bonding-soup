The vocabulary of the P31 architecture.

Decoherence #

When you lose track of your own feelings and opinions under pressure — often without noticing it's happening. You start reflecting what others want instead of what you actually think.

Technical: Loss of authentic internal state under sustained external pressure. In quantum mechanics, the collapse of superposition due to environmental interaction. In P31 psychology: the fawn response — suppression of true self-state to reduce perceived threat.

Floating Neutral #

Figuring out what is true by checking your own internal evidence — rather than waiting for someone else to tell you. Like a circuit that measures its own voltage without being tied to an outside reference.

Technical: From electrical engineering: a node with no defined reference potential, free to self-determine its voltage through internal measurement. In P31: the practice of triangulating ground truth through internal systems rather than external validation. Prevents relational gaslighting from overwriting self-knowledge.

L.O.V.E. #

A score that tracks how much you have connected with and cared for others in the P31 mesh. It can only be earned through real relationships — not bought or transferred.

Technical: Ledger of Ontological Volume and Entropy. A soulbound reputation token earned through authentic connection within the K₄ mesh. Non-transferable. Increases via mutual-care interactions; decays slowly without engagement. Implemented as a Durable Object aggregate in the Cloudflare Workers mesh.

SIC-POVM #

The smallest set of measurements that can fully describe any quantum state. It takes exactly four probes — and those four probes form a tetrahedron. P31 uses this shape as the foundation for its four-node family structure.

Technical: Symmetric Informationally Complete Positive Operator-Valued Measure. The minimum overcomplete measurement frame for a qubit: four rank-1 projectors forming a regular tetrahedron in Bloch-sphere geometry. In P31, the K₄ topology mirrors this structure — four vertices, six edges, every node connected to every other.

K₄ Mesh #

A four-node network where every person is directly connected to every other person. No one is at the center. No one is cut off. The P31 family is structured as a K₄ — four vertices, six edges, total mutual connection.

Technical: The complete graph on four vertices (K₄). Six bidirectional edges; every vertex has degree 3. Structurally identical to a tetrahedron and the SIC-POVM probe geometry. In P31 infrastructure: implemented as a Cloudflare Durable Object cage with per-edge love totals and real-time sync.

Spoon #

A unit of energy or mental capacity. From Spoon Theory: people with chronic illness or disability start each day with a limited number of spoons. Every task costs spoons. When you run out, you're done — recovery takes time. P31 uses spoon counts to size tasks and protect the operator.

Technical: From Christine Miserandino's Spoon Theory (2003). In P31: a discrete unit used to estimate cognitive and executive load in work context documents (WCDs). Tasks are labeled with estimated spoon cost. The operator's daily envelope varies with Ca²⁺ level (hypoparathyroidism, ICD-10 E20.9) and AuDHD executive function capacity.

SOULSAFE #

The P31 safe mode system. When activated, it strips away all visual effects, animations, and complex UI. Named after the Gray Rock method — a way to reduce harm by making yourself a less interesting target. SOULSAFE is a medical accommodation, not just a preference.

Technical: The SOULSAFE / Gray Rock protocol. Triggered by: OS prefers-reduced-motion, URL param ?safe=1, or localStorage['p31-safe-mode'] === 'on'. Applies body.safe-mode class: disables all CSS animations and transitions, hides canvas elements, removes backdrop filters. Required on every P31 surface. Implemented in public/lib/p31-safe-mode.js.

WCD #

A structured task card. WCD stands for Work Context Document. It replaces informal to-do items with a clear format: what you are building, why it matters, what "done" looks like, and how long it will take. Every significant task in P31 has a WCD.

Technical: Work Context Document. A structured dispatch format combining: intent (what + why), scope boundaries (tag-out), spoon estimate, acceptance criteria, and downstream dependencies. Intake surface: /build.html. WCDs are numbered monotonically per product area and never reused for a different intent.

Isostatic Rigidity #

A stable structure that holds its shape by distributing pressure evenly — not by being stiff, but by being balanced. When one part is stressed, the whole system adjusts instead of breaking. P31 infrastructure is designed to be isostatic: if one service fails, the mesh reroutes rather than going dark.

Technical: From structural geology: isostasy is the state where lithospheric pressure is in equilibrium — loads distribute across the crust rather than concentrating at failure points. In P31: the architectural principle that mesh nodes absorb failures via local caching, degraded-mode fallbacks, and Cloudflare KV / Durable Object redundancy rather than hard dependencies on single services.
